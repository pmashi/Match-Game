Welcome to Sand Searcher! You arrived on the sandy shores of a strange land.
In a desparate need for money, you take up the task of searching the sands for lost treasures.
To be a successful in this job you must dig up sand and uncover two of the same items to claim.
If you can find all the hidden items in 16 flips you'll be consider a winner and receive a special title. If you don't, just keep trying you'll become a master searcher in no time!.
Tips:
Your number of flips will be recorded on the left side of the screen as score.
Any achievements you earn may give you a boost in your sand searchering journey.
Just DO NOT reload the page while you're still playing or you'll lose the benefits.
If you wish to play again just click the RESET button to your left.
