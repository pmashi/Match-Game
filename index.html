Welcome to Sand Searcher! a game where you arrive on the sandy shores of a strange land. In a desparate need for money, you take up the task of searching the sands for lost treasure or missing items. To be a successful in this job you must flip over piles of sand(cards) and uncover two cards with the same item to claim that item. If you can find all the hidden items in under 16 flips you will be consider a winner and receive a special title. If you don't, just keep trying you'll become a master searcher in no time!. Tips: The number of flips you have completed will be recorded on the left side of the screen. The reset button will change your location to a different beach in the country.
